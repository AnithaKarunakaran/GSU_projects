What does attrition model tell us about customers?
- customers with declining trend on transaction usage or the number of active users are highly likely to attrite
- customers with more transaction months in past 6 months or with more number of production in transaction are less likely to attrite
- customers who asked for support from customer service on password, product and security issues are less likely to attrite. Customers with billing questions are more likely to attrite
- customers who have been on the books for a long time are likely to attrite
- customers with marketing email bounce back in last 9 months are more likely to attrite. Customers who have clicked on a marketing email in last 9 months are less likely to attrite
